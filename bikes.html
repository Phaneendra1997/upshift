Mahindra MOJO

Engine

300 cc Liquid Cooled Engine With its liquid cooled EFI engine, the Mojo300 ABS pumps out an exhilarating 27 BHP of power and 30 Nm of torque ensuring that every wring of the throttle keeps you zipping ahead.

21 Litre Muscular Fuel Tank

The Mojo’s chiselled 21 litre fuel tank ensures that fuel breaks don’t come in the way of mile munching.

Disc Brakes With Dual Channel ABS

Bring the world to a halt with the 320mm petal disc at the front and the 240mm disc at the rear. Equipped with dual channel ABS, Mojo300 ABS will make sure that it sheds speed as fast as it catches it.

Exhaust

Rock the streets and give your adventures a new soundtrack with a unique and refined deep-bass note of the Dual Barrel exhaust. The Mojo300 ABS is equipped with an exhaust system that will bring the streets alive.
